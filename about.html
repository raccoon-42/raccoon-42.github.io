about me

I'm Ali Özkaya, a computer engineering student at the Izmir Institute of Technology (IZTECH). I build and ship LLM-powered systems end to end: data pipelines, REST APIs, fine-tuned models, and the evaluation around them.

my work spans applied deep learning (parameter-efficient fine-tuning with QLoRA / LoRA, retrieval-augmented generation, and multimodal captioning), plus the software engineering that makes it real: Python services, containerization, automated testing, and reproducible pipelines.

right now I'm an AI developer at the IZTECH Software Society, building a Turkish-language RAG chatbot for academic information. I've also interned on agentic research-and-extraction pipelines and natural-language-to-SQL services.

what I work on

  • language-model fine-tuning & parameter-efficient adaptation (QLoRA / LoRA / DoRA)
  • retrieval-augmented generation, vector search, and knowledge graphs
  • agent & tool orchestration (MCP, OpenAI Agents SDK, LangGraph)
  • building & shipping LLM services (FastAPI, Docker), with a focus on evaluation and reproducibility

education
